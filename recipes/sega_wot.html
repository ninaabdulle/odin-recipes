Sega Wot

Red spicy Ethiopian beef stew.


Ingredients

  • 2-3 lbs beef cubed
  • 5 large onions chopped fine or grated
  • 1 c vegetable oil
  • 5 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 2 teaspoons minced ginger
  • 1/4 c berbere paste
  • 2 teaspoons salt (to taste)
  • 1/2 c. water
  • 1 Tablespoon lemon juice

Method

  1. In a large pot, saute onion, garlic, and ginger in vegetable oil until lightly brown.
  2. Add Bebere and continue to simmer for about 15 minutes at low heat and stirring occasionally.
  3. Add beef and water, let simmer until beef is done.
  4. Add salt and lemon juice and serve hot with injera.
