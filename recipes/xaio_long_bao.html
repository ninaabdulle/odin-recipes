Xiao Long Bao


Xiao Long Bao are delicate Chinese soup dumplings filled with seasoned chicken and savoury broth, steamed to create a tender, juicy bite.

Ingredients

Chicken Jelly:

  • 1kg chicken feet
  • 1 large knob of ginger, roughly broken
  • 10 garlic gloves
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 3 whole spring onions
  • 1 tbsp salt (may need less, add gradually to taste!)
  • 1/2 tbsp pepper (whole peppercorns is best)


Dumplings:

  • 500g chicken mince
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp minced ginger
  • 75g spring onion, finely minced
  • 10g chives, finely minced
  • 1 1/2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tsp Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 500g Chicken jelly from above
  • 3 x packets round dumpling wrappers (90 wrappers)


Method

Chicken Jelly:

  1. Put chickens feet, ginger, garlic gloves, whole spring onions, bay leaves salt and pepper in a pot.
  2. Add in just enough water to cover everything and bring to the boil, scraping off the scum as it forms on top.
  3. Turn down the heat and gently simmer for about 2 hours.
  4. Strain into a container and leave to set overnight in the fridge to solidify.
  5. Once solid, chop the jelly into small pieces and set aside.


Dumplings:

  1. Mix chicken mince, finely chopped spring onion and chives, crushed garlic and crushed ginger, light soy sauce, 2 tsp sesame oil, sugar and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add in chicken jelly and fold through with a spoon.
  3. Pop a teaspoon of the filling in the middle of each dumpling wrapper, wet the edges with water and scrunch together to make a circular shape.
  4. Arrange your dumplings in a lined steamer, cook for around 7 mins and your chicken soup dumplings are ready!
